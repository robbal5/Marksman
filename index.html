Marksman

  • Designed by: Robby Balistreri

How to Play

Marksman is an interactive 2D target shooting game. Users can select their difficulty, and even bring in a friend to play against. Ready, aim, fire!

Controls

The arrow keys control the shot power (up to increase, down to decrease) and trajectory (left to increase, right to decrease). Once the shot is prepared, click space to fire!

Win conditions

Destroy all the targets before running out of shots and you win! (Hit consecutive shots for extra points!)
