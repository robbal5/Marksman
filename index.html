Marksman

  • Designed by: Robby Balistreri

How to Play

Marksman is an interactive shooting game inspired by the classic Tanks arcade game. Users can customize their experience, including the number of targets, shots, and landscape to play on. Ready, aim, fire!

Controls

The arrow keys control the shot power (up to inrease, down to decrease) and trajectory (left and right). Once the shot is prepared, click space to fire!

Win conditions

Destroy all the targets before running out of shots and you win! (Hit consecutive shots for extra points!)
